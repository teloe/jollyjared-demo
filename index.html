    Services

    Beach Rentals

    Heading to the beach but missing the essentials to make it a perfect day? Jolly Jared has the gear you need to make the beach a blast. Orders must be placed before 11:00 A.M. for same day delivery. ​If you need to cancel an order, you must do so before 11:00 A.M. the day of the order or you will be charged the full amount of your order. Your equipment will be dropped off at your location at designated time between 10:00 A.M.-12:00 P.M. We will return at 5:00 P.M.-6:00 P.M. to pick up the equipment.​Daily and weekly rentals are available.

    Coffee Delivery

    A weekly coffee delivery service to several locations in Santa Barbara, including Santa Barbara High School. Jolly Jared has partnered up with a local coffee shop so that you can customize your drink exactly the way you want it, without the hassle of ever leaving your desk. Our custom-built coffee trays are insulated to prevent spilling and keep drinks at the right temperature. Don’t drink coffee, but still want that caffeine fix? We deliver tea and even hot chocolate as well! We are currently working on expanding delivery to new locations, contact us if your business is interested in setting up a weekly service.

    Firewood Sales/ Delivery

    Jolly Jared splits and bundles his own firewood before delivering it directly to your home. With several varieties of wood, each seasoned for a minimum of six months, we guarantee quality firewood that will keep you warm when you need it. We offer small bundles for just $6- perfect for camping or a bonfire on the beach! We also have small kindling starter packs available, see our shop page for more information.
